About Me

Hello! My name is Lindsay Bordelon. Originally from Louisiana, I moved to Colorado seven years ago. I graduated from LSU with a degree in Coastal Environmental Science. I use the environmental science portion of my degree in my role as Environmental, Health and Safety (EHS) Manager for Kinder Morgan. In my role as EHS Manager, I manage applications and databases that store our EHS compliance data. I love how the IT portion of my role allows for creative ways to solve compliance problems.

Thank you for visiting!
